# Contributing to the AI Governance Project

We welcome constructive engagement from researchers, engineers,
policy analysts, and infrastructure architects.

## Areas of Interest

Contributions are particularly valuable in the following areas:

- Enforcement primitives
- Compute governance mechanisms
- Runtime constraint systems
- Cryptographic authorization models
- Sovereign compute regimes
- Identity continuity for persistent AI agents

## How to Contribute

1. Open an issue describing your proposal or critique
2. Reference the relevant note or protocol section
3. Provide architectural reasoning where possible

Focused structural critique is encouraged.

## Collaboration

For deeper architectural collaboration or institutional alignment,
please contact the project lead.
