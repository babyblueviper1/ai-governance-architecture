# Open Research Questions

This project raises several open questions for future work.

## Enforcement Architecture

- How should distributed enforcement nodes coordinate escalation validation?
- What failure modes exist in partially trusted compute environments?

## Identity and Agent Continuity

- How can persistent AI agents maintain verifiable identity across orchestration environments?

## Sovereign Compute Governance

- How should cross-jurisdiction enforcement be coordinated?
- What prevents concentration of enforcement power?

## Economic Signaling

- Should escalation events require economic commitments?
- What mechanisms prevent escalation spam?

These questions represent areas for further research and collaboration.
